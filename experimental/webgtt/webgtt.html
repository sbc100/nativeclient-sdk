Graph Drawing Area

To create a vertex, click once. To select/deselect a vertex, click once on it. To create/select/deselect an edge, select two vertices (one by one). To delete the selected vertices/edges, press the 'Delete' key. Once you have drawn the graph, click on the button below to find a coloring. (Note that for the coloring to be displayed, the status message at the bottom should read 'SUCCESS' (even before clicking the button). If it doesn't, then there is a problem with the application.)

A (vertex) coloring of a graph is an assignment of colors to the vertices of the graph such that NO two vertices that are connected by an edge share a common color.
